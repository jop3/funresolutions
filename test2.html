Gamifying New Year's Resolutions

How Yo's Resolutions Works

Yo's Resolutions is a new year's resolutions app that gamifies your goals to help you stay motivated and achieve your goals faster. To use Yo's Resolutions, simply create an account and add your new year's resolutions. Yo's Resolutions will then break down your resolutions into smaller, more manageable tasks. You can earn points for completing tasks and reach levels. You can also compete with your friends and family to see who can achieve their goals the fastest.
